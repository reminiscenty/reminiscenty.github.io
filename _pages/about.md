I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Santa Barbara, advised by [Prof. Peng Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=QYQUS7gAAAAJ&hl=en&authuser=2).

My research focuses on machine learning and agentic AI, with hands-on experience building and evaluating LLM systems for real-world tasks, including long-context modeling, RAG-style reasoning, and post-training (LoRA/finetuning). I have a strong foundation in reliable machine learning and decision-making under uncertainty, with published work in semi-supervised learning, Bayesian optimization, and conformal prediction at venues such as ICML, AAAI, DAC, and ICCAD.
